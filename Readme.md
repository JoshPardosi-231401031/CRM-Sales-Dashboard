# 📊 CRM Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive **Excel-based dashboard** that tracks B2B sales pipeline performance and agent productivity — built with Power Query for data transformation and Pivot Tables for dynamic analysis.

## 🎯 Project Overview

Sales teams generate massive volumes of pipeline data every quarter, but without proper visualization, critical patterns — stalled deals, underperforming agents, seasonal trends — go unnoticed. This project transforms raw CRM pipeline data into an **interactive Excel dashboard** that gives sales managers the visibility they need to coach teams, forecast revenue, and optimize pipeline health.

The dashboard answers key operational questions:
- **Which sales agents are consistently closing deals, and who needs support?**
- **How does the pipeline flow from prospecting to closed-won across quarters?**
- **What is the revenue distribution across teams and product lines?**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Findings

### 👥 Agent Performance Tracking
- Individual agent metrics are tracked across the full sales pipeline, enabling managers to identify **top performers** and agents who may need additional coaching or territory adjustments.
- Performance comparisons across teams reveal whether success is driven by individual skill, territory quality, or team dynamics.

### 📈 Pipeline Health & Conversion
- The dashboard visualizes deals across all pipeline stages — from prospecting through negotiation to close — highlighting where deals are **stalling or dropping off**.
- This stage-by-stage visibility helps sales leadership focus pipeline reviews on the stages with the highest attrition rates.

### 💰 Quarterly Revenue Trends
- Revenue is tracked across quarters, enabling identification of **seasonal patterns** and more accurate sales forecasting.
- Breakdowns by team and product line reveal which segments are driving growth and which are underperforming relative to targets.

## 🧹 Data Preparation

**Power Query** was used to prepare the raw CRM data for analysis:

- Imported and merged data from multiple source files (`sales_pipeline.csv`, `sales_teams.csv`)
- Cleaned and standardized field formats for consistent analysis across quarters
- Transformed raw pipeline records into structured tables optimized for Pivot Table consumption
